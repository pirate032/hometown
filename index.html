Visit Fredericksburg, VA

Enjoy history, dining and plentiful activities near Washington, DC and Richmond, VA.

Top three activities in Fredericksburg

Visit Civil War battlefields and historic sites

Fredericksburg and the surrounding area are rich in Civil War history.

Grab an ice cream or other great food

Carl's has been a local hangout since 1947, but there is other great food, too!

Enjoy great activities, indoors and out

Throw axes, go boating on the Rappahannock River or zip-lining at a local park.
